The Story Behind It

IT STARTED WITH MY GRANDPA.

Growing up, some of my favorite moments with my Grandpa were when we talked about the games he attended, moments he witnessed, teams he loved.

But over time, I noticed something. The details were fading. The opponent. Who was there with him. The little things that make a memory feel real — slowly going quiet.

Those stories deserved better. And so do yours.

"One day, your kids and grandkids are going to ask what it was like to be there. I Was There makes sure you'll have the answer."
